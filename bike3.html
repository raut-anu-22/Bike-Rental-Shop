JAWA

JAWA is an Indian motorcycle brand that traces its origin to the Czech Republic. The company is known for its classic and vintage style motorcycles that have a loyal fan base. JAWA motorcycles are powered by single-cylinder or twin-cylinder engines that offer a smooth and refined ride.

Some of the popular models of JAWA are:

  • JAWA - The original model that has a retro design and a 293 cc engine.
  • JAWA 42 - The modern model that has a sporty look and a 293 cc engine.
  • JAWA Perak - The bobber model that has a minimalist design and a 334 cc engine.
  • JAWA Yezdi - The upcoming model that will revive the iconic Yezdi brand and will have a 250 cc engine.
  • JAWA Adventure - The upcoming model that will be an adventure-tourer and will have a 334 cc engine.
